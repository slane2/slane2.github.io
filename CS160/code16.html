What do the prefix names kilo, mega, giga, tera, and peta represent in bytes and what were the Greek origins of the words?

Kilo means thousand in greek and it means 1024 in bytes. Mega in greek means great and represents 1,048,576 or 2^20 bytes. Giga in greek is giant and represents 1024 megabytes. Tera is monster in greek and represents 2^40th bytes. Peta bytes is 2^50th bytes.
