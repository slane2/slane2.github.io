What is term “edge cases” referring to in software development?

Obscure and unlikely things that a user may try to do with a program that will likely reveal a bug.

Summarize briefly Linus Torvalds advice about “large projects” given in 2004

Start small and focus on one detail at a time. If it doesn't solve a fairly immediate need it's almost certainly over designed.
