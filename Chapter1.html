What roles in the Chandler project did Michael Toy, John Anderson, Ted Burgess, Mitchell Kapor, and Lou Montulli hold?
Michael Toy was the project manager. John Anderson was the lead programmer. Mitchell Kapor was the founder and funder of OSAF. Everyone else was a programmer.
