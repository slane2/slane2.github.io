Linus Torvalds used a “science” and “witchcraft” analogy referring to software, explain.
He said that traditional software was to modern, open source software in the way that witchcraft compared to science. Witchcraft is a small carefully guarded secret and science is a system that builds on peer analysis. Science has phased out witchcraft for the same reason that open source software has mostly phased out traditional software.
